    Contents

    English

    1
    • (fol. 1)
    • Treatise on diet and astrology ('Gouernayle of helth').
    • (incipit) (preface)In this treteez that ys called Gouernayle of helth
    • (incipit) (text) <H>yt nedyth hym pat wol lyue long in hole of body
    • (explicit)A worthy doctor made pis litel tretees after pe descripcion of dyvers doctoures s. Bernard, Austyn, Plate et Tholome, Arystotes et Galyen, Cydrac and Avycen and mony oder doctoris acordyth with pe same.
    • English
    2
    • (fol. 39v)
    • Note: Cryptogram, with key, of owner's name (see under provenance)
    • English
    3
    • (fol. 40)
    • Table giving the astrological significance of the letters of the alphabet
    • Note: (in a contemporary hand)
    • English
    4
    • (fol. 40v)
    • Astrological wheel
    • Note: (in a contemporary hand)
    • English

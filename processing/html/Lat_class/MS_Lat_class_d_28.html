History

Origin:
15th century, before 1449
Italian

Provenance and Acquisition: On fol. 80v is a letter dated Perusie, 5 Kal.Jul.1449, in which the writer offers his services to Antonius. On fol.97v – 98 are pen trials in various types of script, among them ‘Illustrissimo et excellentissimo domino Antonio de Manfredi de Florentia’. There are marginalia in a contemporary hand. Given by Prof. A. C. Clark , 1935.

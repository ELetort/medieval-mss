  • Note: Ends imperf. at the beginning of the tenth precept; preceded (fol.10) by a table of contents of which only the last leaf remains. The prefatory section ‘Of holy poverte’ (fol.11 – 17) is in the version found also in Lichfield Cathedral MS. 5. This MS. was used by Pynson for his edition of 1493, and the text has been marked off in sections corresponding to the pages of the edition; see M.Morgan, The Library, 5th ser., vol.8, pp. 217 – 228, with reproductions.

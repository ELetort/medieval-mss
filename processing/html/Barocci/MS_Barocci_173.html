    Physical Description

    • Format: codex
      • Support: Material: perg

        Material Support: parchment

        Extent: Dimensions (ruled): 220 × 150 mm.

        Collation: (fol. 1) i–xii 8, (fol. 97) xiii 10 (6 canc.), (fol. 106) xiv–xvi 8, (fol. 130) xvii 3 leaves, (fol. 133) xviii–xx 8.

      • Layout:

        Ruling: 31 lines. Ruling type similar to Leroy 42C1q.

    • Hand(s): Two scribes. A wrote fols. 1r–48v, 57r–64v, 73r–97r, 102r–132v; B wrote fols. 97r–101r. Leaves 49–56, 65–72, 133–56 are replacements in a 15th-century hand. The original leaves 49–56 are now fols. 61–8 in Laurentianus 56. 13; the original leaves 65–72 are now in Leipzig, Univ.-Bibl. gr. 46 (cf. B, ‘Contents’, ad fin.).

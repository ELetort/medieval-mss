Summary

Summary: Scholar’s Notebooks 16th or 17th cent.

Contents

1
  • (fols. 1r–434b verso)
  • Collection of scholar’s notebooks on Plato and Aristotle,
  • Note: mainly on the latter’s Categoriae and De interpretatione, with copious extracts from the commentaries of Ammonius. The heading on fol. 142r runs: ἐκ τῶν Ἀμμωνίου Ἑρμείου ὑπομνημάτων εἰϲ τὸ περὶ ἑρμηνείαϲ Ἀριϲτοτέλουϲ. Πέτρου Οὐικτορίου ϲφαλμάτων ἐπανορθώματα.
  • Greek
  • Greek and Italian

Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: perg

      Material Support: Parchment (except fols. i–iv and 100–124). Fols. 102–109 are damaged, fols. 110–121 are 15th cent. replacements (unwatermarked paper).

      Extent: iv + 124 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 160 × 115 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 110 × 75 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–ii), (iii–iv), 1–43, 45–59, 60ab, 61–109, (110–121), (122), (123–4).

      Collation: Collation of fols. 1–109: (fol. 1) i 10 (6, 7, 8 canc.), (fol. 8) ii–iii 8, (fol. 24) iv 10 (2, 8 canc.), (fol. 32) v–vi 8, (fol. 47) vii 10 (3, 9 canc.), (fol. 55) viii 8, (fol. 62) ix 10 (2, 8 canc.), (fol. 70) x–xiv 8. Quires numbered on bottom left corner of first recto.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 21–3 lines, ruling type Leroy 20C1.

  • Hand(s): One scribe.
  • Decoration:

    Many pages have spaces left for illuminations; the miniatures, mostly in a poor condition now, were painted only as far as fol. 31v.

    Miniatures. Ornamental strips. (Hutter ii. 2, Abb. 106-137)

  • Additions: 1 (fols. iii verso–iv recto, fol. 122v) prayers, etc., damaged; illiterate 15th cent. hand.
  • Binding:

    Brown-stamped brown leather, early 18th cent.

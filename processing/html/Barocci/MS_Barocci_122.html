Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: perg
      Extent: iii + 224 leaves.Dimensions (leaf): 235 × 160 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 180 × 110 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–iii), 1–223, (224).

      Collation: (fol. 1) i 5 leaves, (fol. 6) ii 8, (fol. 14) iii 10 (2, 8 canc.), (fol. 22) iv 10 (2, 8 canc.), (fol. 30) v 8, (fol. 38) vi 10 (3, 7 canc.), (fol. 46) vii 8, (fol. 54) viii 11 (2, 7 canc.), (fol. 63) ix 8, (fol. 71) x 10 (2, 7 canc., 4 missing), (fol. 78) xi–xix 8, (fol. 150) xx 10 (3, 7 canc.), (fol. 158) xxi–xxvii 8, (fol. 214) xxviii 10. Quires numbered in top centre margin of first recto and bottom right of last verso.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 36–46 lines. Ruling type Leroy 20C1.

  • Hand(s): Text written probably by three scribes. A: fols. 1r–5v, 59rv, 94r–223v. B: fols. 6r–54v, 55v–58r, 61v. C: fols. 54v–55r, 58v, 60r–61r, 62rv.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: lemmata and initials in faded red; simple penwork ornament.

  • Additions: 1 fol. 5v, obituary of 1362: μηνὶ ἰουνίῳ κδʹ ἡμέρᾳ παραϲκευῇ τὸ γενέϲιον τοῦ ἁγίου ἐνδόξου προδρόμου καὶ βαπτιϲτοῦ Ἰωάννου ὥρᾳ θʹ τῆϲ ἡμέραϲ ἐκοιμή<θη> ἡ δούλη τοῦ θεοῦ Μαρία ἡ Μακρυδούκενα ἡ διὰ τοῦ θείου καὶ ἀγγελικοῦ ϲχήματοϲ μετοναμϲθεῖϲα Μάρθα μοναχή ἔτουϲ ͵ϛου ωου οʹ ἰνδικτιῶνοϲ ιεηϲ. 2 fol. 7r μαρ.κ. δοῦκαϲ ο̣μαρου. 3 fol. 22v some cryptography. 4 fol. 46r astrological note εἰ θέλοιϲ εὑρεῖν τὸν θεμέλιον τῆϲ ϲελήνηϲ. 5 fol. iiv possibly the draft of a letter, ὦ ἐπὶ διανοίαϲ ϲε περίφρων.Numerous pentrials on fols. ii–iii. Fol. 1r has the no. ‘30’.A loose sheet of paper written by an uncount 15th or 16th cent. hand contains unidentified theological matter.A few marginalia by the scribes, more added by a 14th cent. hand.
  • Binding:

    Binding of blind-stamped brown leather over wood, East European with Greek spine, 15th–16th cent.; hunting-scene stamps; clasps now missing.

Summary

Summary:A, E, F 13th cent. (middle to late); B, C, D late 13th or 14th cent.

Physical Description

  • Composite: A, B, C, D, E, F
  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Oriental paper. Fol. 1 has had the top right-hand corner cut off.

      Extent: iii + 298 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 172 × 127 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–iii), 1–296, (297–8); fols. i–iii and 297–8 blank.

  • Additions: On fol. 1r illegible 14th cent. note, perhaps listing contents.On fol. 257v a heading, perhaps intended for D item 1, ἐπιϲτολαὶ ϲχεδιαϲθεῖϲαι Κυρίλλου μοναχοῦ καὶ ἀδελφιδοῦ τοῦ πανιερωτάτου μητροπολίτου.E 3: addition at end: τὸ δὲ ὅπου ἐϲτὶν ἡ βαϲιλεία καὶ ἡ δύναμιϲ καὶ ἡ δόξα, ἔν τιϲιν οὐ κεῖται μέχρι τοῦ ἀμήν.
  • Binding:

    Brown leather blind stamped, some stamps showing hunting-scene; perhaps Italian but in the Greek style; 16th cent., spine modern.

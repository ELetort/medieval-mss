Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Paper, watermarked (among other designs cf. Mošin–Trajlić no. 377 (AD 1385–95) and nos. 5171–2 (AD 1350–70)).

      Extent: 138 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 210 × 135 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 150 × 95 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (1–4), (5–16), 17–133, (134–8).

      Collation: Collation of fols. 15–133: (fol. 15) i 8 (1, 2 substitute leaves), (fol. 23) ii–xiv 8, (fol. 127) xv 8 (8 missing). Traces of original quire signatures on lower right corner of first recto.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 29 lines, ruling type Leroy 00C1.

  • Hand(s): One scribe.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: titles and initials in vermilion.

  • Additions: fols. 5–16 were added in the late 15th or early 16th cent: paper, unwatermarked with a design not clearly identifiable: contains (fols. 6r–11v) ἀποφθέγματα διαφόρων ποιητῶν, πρεϲβύτατον τῶν ὄντων θεόϲ, ἔτι ἄνδρεϲ ἦϲαν οἱ ταῦτα νομοθετήϲαντεϲ οὐ γυναῖκεϲ. Fols. 5, 12, 14 blank.
  • Binding:

    Italian Renaissance binding, no. 2755 in T. de Marinis, La Legatura artistica in Italia nei secoli xv e xvi, Florence 1960.

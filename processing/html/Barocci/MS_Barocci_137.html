Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Oriental paper (except fols. 296–323, 15th cent., watermarked with design not in the repertoria).

      Extent: i + 323 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 235 × 165 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 175 × 110 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i), 1–295, (296–323).

      Collation: (fol. 1) i–iii 8, (fol. 25) iv 5 leaves, (fol. 30) v 8, (fol. 38) vi 7 leaves, (fol. 45) vii–xxviii 8, (fol. 221) xxix 7 leaves, (fol. 228) xxx 9 leaves, (fol. 237) xxxi–xxxiv 8, (fol. 269) xxxv 1, (fol. 270) xxxvi–xxxviii 8, (fol. 294) xxxix 2, (fol. 296) xl 8, (fol. 304) lxi 14. Quires numbered on the bottom right centre margin of the first recto.

    • Layout:

      29 ruled lines. Ruling type Leroy 32C1.

  • Hand(s): One scribe.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: titles, headings and initials in vermilion and carmine.

  • Additions: Interlinear glosses of Renaissance date on fols. 1–6 and occasionally elsewhere.
  • Binding:

    Binding of blind-stamped brown leather over wood, late Byzantine style, 15th–16th cent.; five metal ornaments on each panel, remains of clasps. A piece of paper on the spine has the no. ‘137’ twice and ‘Plutarchi Vitae / Arriani de exped. Alex.’.

Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Oriental paper.

      Extent: iii + 327 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 360 × 270 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 260 × 190 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–iii), 1–324, (325–7).

      Collation: (fol. 7) i–xxxix 8, (fol. 319) xl 6 leaves. Some quires numbered on bottom right corner of first recto and bottom left corner of last verso, others on top right corner of first recto.

    • Layout:

      Columns: 2
      Ruling: 2 columns (except fols. 1–6) of 38 lines.

  • Hand(s): Two scribes; A fols. 7r–14v, 67v–324r; B fols. 1r–6v, 15r–67v. Some marginalia by the scribes, partly in vermilion.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: titles, headpieces and initials, generally in carmine.

    Ornamental headpieces and decorated initials at the beginnings of homilies. (Hutter iii. 122, Abb. 458-465)

  • Binding:

    Binding of blind-stamped brown leather, 17th–18th cent.

History

Origin:
13th century, end (Hutter); Late 13th or early 14th cent. (Wilson)

Provenance and Acquisition: Possession note on fol. 7r: βίβλοϲ ἱερὰ τῆϲ μονῆϲ τῶν ῥιζῶν πληϲίον τευχίων (more likely reading that τειχῶν).

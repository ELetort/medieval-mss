Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: perg

      Material Support: Parchment (except fols. i–iii and 285).

      Extent: v + 285 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 160 × 110 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 115 × 80 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–iii), (iv), v, 1–284, (285).

      Collation: (fol. v) i–xxi 8, (fol. 168) xxii 12 (3, 9 canc.), (fol. 178) xxiii–xxxii 8, (fol. 258) xxxiii 6, (fol. 264) xxxiv 8, (fol. 272) xxxv 10, (fol. 282) xxxvi 3 leaves. Quires numbered bottom left of first recto and bottom right of last verso.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 22 lines. Ruling type similar to Leroy 44D1.

  • Hand(s): Two scribes: A fol. v recto and verso, fols. 1r–127v, 263r–284r; B 128r–263r.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: titles and initials in faded carmine, simple penwork headpieces.

  • Additions: Subscription fol. 283r, see above. Below in later hand ἔτουϲ ϡηʹ.Fols. 283r–284v also have pentrials.
  • Binding:

    Blind-stamped brown leather, 17th–18th cent.

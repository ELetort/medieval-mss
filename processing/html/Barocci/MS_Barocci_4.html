Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Paper, Oriental and Western, some leaves watermarked (one design unidentified, the others may be Watermark: no. 3356 in Mošin–Traljić (AD 1334), and Watermark: no. 3588 ibid. (AD 1362)).

      Extent: viii + 222 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 140 × 105 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 100 × 70 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–ii), (iii–viii), 1–215, (216–222); fols. i recto–vii recto and 212v–222v blank except for pentrials.

      Collation: Collation of fols. 1–215: (fol. 1) i 8 (disordered), (fol. 9) ii–xiii 8, (fol. 105) xiv 7 leaves, (fol. 112) xv–xxvii 8. Quires numbered on bottom right corner of first recto (the numbers may be later additions).

    • Layout:

      Ruling not visible. Column of 14 lines by scribe A, 19 by B.

  • Hand(s):

    Two scholarly hands. A: fols. 1r–8v, B: fols. 9r–211v. Copious glosses by A, a few marginalia by B. It is possible that the sheets written by A are a fragment of a separate book, which B made the beginning of his own text.

  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: a penwork heading on fol. 1r in brown and carmine; titles and initials in faded carmine or vermilion.

  • Additions: fol. 212r: a few lines of unidentified text in a hand similar to B, beg. ἦν ὅτε ἐγένοντο λόγοι, ending illegible.
  • Binding:

    Binding of brown-stamped brown leather, 18th cent. (?).

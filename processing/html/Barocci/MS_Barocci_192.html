Summary

Summary: Vitae Sanctorum 14th cent.

Physical Description

  • Composite: fols. 3-321 (main MS.) || fols. 322-5 (flyleaves)
  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Paper (except fols. iv and 322–5), of Western manufacture (except probably fols. 1–2), watermarked (designs not identifiable).

      Extent: iv + 328 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 290 × 200 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 215 × 140 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–iii) (iv) (1–2) 3–151, 152ab, 153–9, 160ab, 161–321, (322–5) (326–8).

      Collation: (fol. 3) i–v 8, (fol. 43) vi 10 (4 canc.), (fol. 52) vii–viii 8, (fol. 68) ix 10 (8 canc.), (fol. 77) x–xvii 8, (fol. 141) xviii 10 (5 canc.), (fol. 150) xix 12, (fol. 160b) xx 9, (fol. 169) xxi–xxxii 8, (fol. 273) xxxiii 10, (fol. 283) xxxiv–xxxvi 8, (fol. 307) xxxvii 10 (1 canc.), (fol. 316) xxxviii 6. Quires numbered on bottom of last verso. Fol. 75 is probably a substitute leaf, 14th cent.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 38 lines.

  • Hand(s): Two scribes, A fols. 3r–48r, 118v–306r, 316r–321v; B fols. 48r–188v, 306v–315r.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: simple penwork ornament in vermilion and brown; some initials in vermilion.

  • Binding:

    Blind-stamped brown leather, 17th - 18th cent.

  • Accompanying Material: Flyleaves:

    1 fol. iv: parchment; some notes on recto, perhaps accounts; note on verso: εἰϲ τὸν μήναν τὸν λεγόμενον νοέμριον εἰϲ τεϲ ὑκοϲὴ πέντε ἐπήγα οἰϲ τὸ.βὴδ̣άλετάτουλου ζευγαρηα ιγʹ γροϲαθ α̣κ̣ο̣μη ὰργά̣ π̣ϲ̣ εʹ προϲϲὸλδια. ἀκ̣ομη ζευγάρηα β .. β . 14th–15th cent. (?).

    2 fols. 1–2, paper, 14th cent., fragments from a lectionary.

    3 fols. 322–5: see part 2 below

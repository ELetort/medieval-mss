Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Oriental paper (fols. 1–49); Western paper, watermarked but with a design that cannot be identified (fols. i–xxxviii, 50–59).

      Extent: xxxviii + 59 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 172 × 117 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 130 × 85 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–xxxviii), 1–49, (50–9).

      Collation: (fol. 1) i 1 leaf, (fol. 2) ii 8, (fol. 10) iii 8 (2, 7 missing), (fol. 16) iv 6, (fol. 22) v 8, (fol. 30) vi 8 (3, 6 missing), (fol. 36) vii 6, (fol. 42) viii 8; the book is incomplete at both ends. Quire numbers not original.

      Condition: Note: This MS. used to contain a grammatical fragment and 13 letters of Tzetzes, as listed in Coxe’s catalogue, but there is no trace of them now, and on the question of authorship see C. Wendel, RE s.v. Tzetzes, col. 2006.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 20 lines.

  • Hand(s): Text written by one hand of the scholarly type. Various pentrials by later owners. Fols. 48–9 partially repaired by a 15th cent. hand.
  • Binding:

    Binding of blind-stamped brown leather over wood, in late Byzantine style, 15th–16th cent.; hunting-scene stamps.

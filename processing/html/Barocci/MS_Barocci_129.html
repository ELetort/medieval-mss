Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Oriental paper (except fol. 150).

      Extent: iii + 152 leaves.Dimensions (leaf): 255 × 170 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–iii), 1–149, (150), (151–2).

      Collation: Collation of fols. 1–149: (fol. 1) i–xviii 8, (fol. 145) xix 5 leaves.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: usually 28–9 lines. Ruling type Leroy 20C1. Dimensions (ruled): 205 × 135 mm.

  • Hand(s): One scribe.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: headpiece of simple penwork ornament, brown and vermilion; vermilion initials. (Hutter iii. 144, Abb. 528)

  • Additions: Marginalia occasionally by 15th cent. owner, who also wrote a few theological notes on fol. 150v, a sheet of Western paper now glued onto a later sheet inserted by a binder.
  • Binding:

    Binding modern

Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Paper (except fols. 1 and 124) Watermark: watermarked (three designs, (a) similar to Mošin–Trajlić 6956, AD 1338–40 Watermark: (b) unidentifiable Watermark: (c) not in the repertoria, but some similarity to Mošin–Trajlić 6844, AD 1343)

      Extent: 124 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 215 × 70 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 150 × 70 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (1), 2–123, (124).

      Collation: fols. 2–123: (fol. 2) i–v 8, (fol. 42) vi 6, (fol. 48) vii–xiv 8, (fol. 112) xv–xvi 6. Quires numbered on centre bottom margin of first recto and last verso.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 24–7 lines. Ruling type similar to Leroy 44D1.

  • Hand(s): One scholarly hand; ἀνάγκη is frequently abbreviated ἀνᾱ. Scholia and interlinear notes, mainly by the scribe, a few in other hands, all probably of the 14th cent. Variants from other MS(S). frequently noted by γρ. At 216b17 (fol. 49r) a long marginal note begins: ἰϲτέον ὅτι τὸ παρὸν ..... οὐκ ἐξηγοῦνται οὔτε ὁ Ϲυμπλίκιοϲ οὔτε ὁ Φιλόπονοϲ Ἰωάννηϲ. ἢ τοίνυν ἐν τοῖϲ παρ’ αὐτοῖϲ ἀντιγράφοιϲ οὐκ ἦν κείμενον τὸ παρόν, ἢ ὑπ’ ἄλλου τινὸϲ ὕϲτερον προϲτεθεῖται. ὅμωϲ δὲ ἐξηγητέον καὶ ϲαφηνιτέον ἐϲ δύναμιν τῷ φιλοϲοφωτάτῳ διδαϲκάλῳ κατακολουθοῦνταϲ τῷ ὁϲιωτάτῳ Δαμιανῷ.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: titles, initials and a few of the scholia in faded red.

  • Additions: Inside the back cover ‘Aristotelis physica’.
  • Binding:

    Binding of blind-stamped brown leather, 15th–16th cent., perhaps Venetian.

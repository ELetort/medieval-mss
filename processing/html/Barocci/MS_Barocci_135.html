Summary

Summary: Gregorius Nazianzenus AD 1305

Physical Description

  • Composite: two parts
  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Western paper, watermarked (the only identifiable design being similar to Mošin–Trajlić no. 3400, but this is dated c.1390).

      Extent: iii + 251 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 222 × 145 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 150 × 95 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–iii), (1–16), (17–21), 22–147, 148ab, 149–248, (249–51); fols. 1–16, 17–21 are substituted leaves, the former having a watermark similar to Mošin–Trajlić no. 4693, AD 1375.

      Collation: (fol. 22) i–xxviii 8, (fol. 245) xxix 4 leaves.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 22 lines.

  • Hand(s): Written by Manuel Pancratius ; subscription fol. 248v: ἐτελειώθη ἡ παροῦϲα βίβλοϲ διὰ χειρὸϲ ἁμαρτωλοῦ Μανουὴλ τοῦ Παγκ̣ρ̣ατίου μηνὶ ἰαννουρίῳ κε ἡμέρᾳ κβε ἰνδικτιῶνοϲ γ̣. For interpretation see Vogel–Gardthausen p. 280.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: headings, titles and initials in vermilion.

  • Additions:
  • Binding:

    Binding modern. On top fore-edge † ΘΕΟΛΟΓΟϹ.

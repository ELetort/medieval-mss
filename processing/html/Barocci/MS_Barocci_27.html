Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Oriental paper.

      Extent: iii + 335 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 178 × 122 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–ii), (iii, 1–4), 5–324, (325–35).

      Collation: (fol. 5) i–vii 10, (fol. 75) viii 10 leaves, (fol. 85) ix–xiii 10, (fol. 135) xiv–xv 12, (fol. 159) xvi–xviii 10, (fol. 189) xix 12, (fol. 201) xx–xxviii 10, (fol. 291) xxxix–xxx 12, (fol. 315) xxxi 10 (disordered). Incomplete at beginning. Fols. i recto–iii recto, fols. 123rv, 124r, 325r–end blank. Some quires numbered on the bottom right hand corner of the last verso.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 26–8 lines. Ruling type Leroy 00C1. Dimensions (ruled): 125 × 75 mm.

  • Hand(s): Text written by Romanos Lamia, chartophlyax; Vogel–Gardthausen, p. 394, do not list this MS., but the identification is assured by the use of the very rare compendium ↄ for αν.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: Initials in faded carmine.

  • Additions:

    1 fol. iii verso: index of contents of another MS., 15th cent.

    2 fol. 1rv, Gregorius Nyssenus, excerpts from Quid sit ad imaginem dei (cf. PG 44. 1327–46), beg. ἐκ τῶν καθ’ ἡμᾶϲ ἔϲτι γνῶναι, ends τοῦτο ἐϲτὶ τὸ κατ’ εἰκόνα Θεοῦ. 14th cent.

    3 fols. 1v–2v, same hand as no. 2, <Ephraem Syrus> , de virtutibus et passionibus, pr. F. Assemani, Rome 1732–46, vol. iii, pp. 425 ff., breaks off at 426F with ἡ κατάκυτοϲ ἀγρία.

    4 fol. 3rv, 15th cent. index of contents.

    5 fol. 4rv some prayers, 14th cent.

    6 fol. 124v, 15th cent. index of contents.—Marginalia by the scribe and two 15th cent. owners, mainly indications of content. Unintelligible cryptography on fols. 58r and 85r by another later owner.

  • Binding:

    Binding of brown leather, 18th cent. (?).

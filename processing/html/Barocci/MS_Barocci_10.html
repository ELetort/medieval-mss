    Physical Description

    • Format: codex
      • Support: Material: chart

        Material Support: Western paper. Watermark similar to design 3356 in Mošin–Traljić (AD 1334).

        Extent: Dimensions (leaf): 150 × 105 mm.

        Collation: (fol. 70) i missing, ii–viii 8, (fol. 126) ix 4, (fol. 130) remainder uncertain, but one leaf has fallen out between fols. 139 and 140. Quires numbered in lower centre margin of first recto and last verso.

        Ruling: 16-21 lines. Dimensions (ruled): 100 × 70 mm.

      Rubrication: titles, headings and initials in vermilion.

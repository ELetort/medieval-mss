  • Note: fols. 1r–102v contain the readings for movable feasts, as pr. in the Venice Anagnosticon of 1596, fols. 73–174; fols. 103r–131v the fixed feasts, as ibid., fols. 3–72, breaking off in the penultimate line of fol. 72v with καὶ διωδεύϲαμεν ἐρήμουϲ ἀβά[τουϲ. In the edition of the Prophetologion by C. Hoeg and G. Zuntz = Monumenta Musicae Byzantinae, Lectionaria I, Copenhagen 1939–, this MS. has the siglum Bc.

Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Paper, watermarked with a design not identifiable.

      Extent: 293 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 295 × 205 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 210 × 140 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (1–2), 3–159, 160ab, 161–87, 188ab, 189–291, (292–3).

      Collation: (fol. 3) i 6, (fol. 9) ii–v 8, (fol. 41) vi–vii 4, (fol. 49) viii–xxiv 8, (fol. 184) xxv 6, (fol. 189) xxvi–xxix 8, (fol. 221) xxx 10, (fol. 231) xxxi 4, (fol. 235) xxxii 12, (fol. 247) xxxiii 4, (fol. 251) xxxiv 12, (fol. 263) xxxv 4, (fol. 267) xxxvi 12, (fol. 279) xxxvii 4, (fol. 283) xxxviii 9 leaves. A few quires are numbered on the top right corner of the first recto.

    • Layout:

      Ruling 34–8 lines; various ruling types.

  • Hand(s): All written by one scribe in a rather variable hand, with the possible exception of fols. 3–8.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: titles, initials and simple penwork ornament in vermilion, often faded.

  • Binding:

    Binding of blind-stamped dark-brown leather, 17th–18th cent.

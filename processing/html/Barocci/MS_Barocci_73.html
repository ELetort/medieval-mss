Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Paper, watermarked with various designs (cf. inter alia Mošin–Trajlić nos. 2669 (AD 1340–2) and 1594 (AD 1356)).

      Extent: i + 263 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 210 × 140 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i), 1–262, (263).

      Collation: (fols. 1–6, 23) i 8 (1 missing), (fols. 7–22) ii–iii 8, (fol. 24) iv–xxxii 8, (fol. 256) xxxiii 8 (8 missing). Some quires numbered on bottom of centre margin on last verso.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 24 lines. Dimensions (ruled): 150 × 80 mm.

  • Hand(s): One scribe, who wrote a different style of hand on fol. 114r.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: titles and initials in faded red.

  • Additions: Glosses, variants and marginalia. On fols. 8v and 209r there is a note on πλημελεῖν: ὁ μὲν Θεϲϲαλονίκηϲ οὕτω γράφει δι’ ἑνὸϲ /μ/.

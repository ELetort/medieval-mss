Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Oriental paper.

      Extent: iii + 150 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 162 × 113 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 130 × 75 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–ii), (iii), 1–9, 11–148, (149–50).

      Collation: (Fols. 148, 1–6) i 8 (1 missing), (fols. 7–9, 11–15) ii 8, (fol. 17) iii–v 8, (fols. 16, 41–7) vi 8, (fols. 48–135) vii–xvii 8; after fol. 135 a lacuna; remainder uncertain. Some quires numbered on centre bottom margin of first recto and/or last verso.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 20–30 lines.

  • Hand(s): One scholarly hand, which shows a variant form on fols. 136r–141r.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: in item 1 initials and marginal indications of contents in vermilion; in item 4 some diagrams in vermilion.

  • Additions: A few 16th cent. marginalia.
  • Binding:

    Blind-stamped brown leather, 17th–18th cent.

Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: perg

      Material Support: Parchment (except fols. i–iv and 49).

      Extent: iv + 49 leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 280 × 190 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 220 × 130 mm.

      Foliation: Foliated (i–ii) (iii–iv), 1–48, (49).

      Collation: fols. 1–48: (fol. 5) i (probably 8, disordered), (fol. 40) ii 8 (1, 8 missing), (fol. 9) iii–v 8, (fol. 33) vi 8 (3 canc.), (fol. 46) vii (3 leaves). Quires numbered on the bottom right corner of the first recto.

    • Layout:

      Ruling: 30 lines, ruling type Leroy 02C1.

  • Hand(s): One scribe.
  • Decoration:

    Rubrication: title, simple penwork ornament and lemmata from Hippocrates in carmine.

  • Additions: A very few marginalia and glosses, various dates. See also above, items 1-3, 5.
  • Binding:

    Binding modern.

Contents

Summary of Contents: Manuscript fragments and documents accumulated by N. R. Ker (1908-82) , from known sources, 1933-65 and n.d. (the quotations of soucres below are from notes in Ker's hand). 68 leaves (fol. 67 arrived in two parts).

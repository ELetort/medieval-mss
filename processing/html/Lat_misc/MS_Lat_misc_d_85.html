Physical Description

  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: perg

      Material Support: parchment

      Extent: i (paper) + 126 + i (paper) Dimensions (leaf): 255 × 165 mm.
      Dimensions: 162-4 × 81-3 (89-91 incl. left bounding lines) mm.

      Foliation: Contemporary foliation in pink ink, in roman numerals, the first character between the pair of right-hand vertical bounding lines: xlj-lxii, 63, lxiiii-lxxxvi, lxxxiii-clxvi; half of fols. cviii and cx excised, including the foliation; one preceding flyleaf foliated in modern pencil, as is final original parchment leaf, and a flyleaf, foliated i and 167-168

    • Layout:

      Columns: 1 2
      Written Lines: 21 23
      21 lines; but occasionally over-running to ruling to give up to 23 lines on a page; in 1, sometimes 2 column(s)

  • Hand(s):

    Humanistic capitals and italic script, in brown and pink inks. Written by Bartolomeo Fonzio.

  • Decoration:

    Fine miniatures (mostly wash drawings). In the style of the school of Botticelli. (P&A ii. 329, pl. XXX)

    Miniatures depicting a Castel sant'Angelo, Rome (fol. 63r), an elephant (fol. 72v), a giraffe (fol. 73r), classical capitals, arcades and other classical remains and sculptures and inscriptions seen in Rome (fols. 132v-153r, 161v)

  • Binding:

    Bound in plain parchment over thin pasteboards; the spine inscribed 'Noti|zie | Belle | e | nece[...] | [...] | Anti[qu?..] | di | Roma'; the brown leather case lettered in gilt on the spine 'COLLECTANEA | EPIGRAPHICA | SAECULI XV.'

  • Summary Catalogue no.: Not in SC (late accession)

Contents

Summary of Contents: Collection of fragments from the Bliss & Phillipps collections including St. Paul, Epistle to the Romans with Commentary of Gilbert de la Porrée (fol. 26), Flemish (?), 12th century, end. The fragments are arranged as follows: (fols. 1-21) fragments from books probably bound at Oxford (also probably from Oxford bindings are C.m, y, z, and MS. Lat. misc. d. 13 (30572) fols. 1–2); (fols 22-57) miscellaneous; (fols. 58-109) liturgical; and (fols. 110-127) legal.

Physical Description

  • Secundo Folio: secundum modum
  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: perg

      Material Support: parchment

      Extent: 213 leavesDimensions (leaf): 265 × 190 mm.
      Dimensions (written): 190–205 × 120 mm.

      Collation: 1(8)–8(8), catchwords, quires 1–7 numbered at the end; + 1(8) (wants 1; preceded by an unnumbered quaternion), 2(8)–8(8), 9(8) (+2 added at the end), catchwords, the quires numbered [1]–9 at the end; + 1(8)–5(8), 6(6), 7(8), 8(6), 9(4), 10(4) (+1 added between 3 and 4; 4 canc.), 11(3)

      Condition: lower right hand corner severely mutilated

    • Layout:

      40–43 long lines

  • Decoration:

    Border, initial (mutilated) (P&A iii. 737)

    illuminated initial and border in blue, gold, red and white (fol.1), the rest blue flourished in red and violet (fol.157v).

  • Accompanying Material: Fol.213 is a leaf of a 14th cent. Sarum missal containing parts of the ‘ordo ad cathecuminum faciendum’ (edition of W. Legg, pp.124, 5) and prayers for the dead (ibid., pp.431, 441, 449).

Contents

1
  • Notarial instruments,
  • Note: Minutes (imbreviaturae) of notarial instruments drawn up by Petrus Bertolaxii Belli in the years 1394–6. Each quire is headed by the words ‘Hos sequentes imbreviaturas scripsi rogatus etc. Ego Petrus Bertolaxii Belli de Albino imperiali auctoritate publicus Pergamensis notarius missus regalis iudex ordinarius’, with the year and his notarial sign.

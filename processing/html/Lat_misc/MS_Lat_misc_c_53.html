Summary:Statutes of Pisa. Italian, 16th century, third quarter.

Contents

  • Statutes of Pisa, 1323-1515
  • (rubric) Statuta nova civitatis Pisar<um> del 1323 sino 1515
  • Latin and Italian
    • (fol.1)
    • Note: Statutes 1323–1409, followed (fol.10v) by a note on Pisan history, an autobiographical note by the scribe, Joannes olim Baptiste quondam Joannis Guarnerii, notary of Pisa, and two decrees in Italian, one dated 1551.
    • (fol.12)
    • (rubric) Statuta novissima civitatis Pis<arum>
    • Note: dated 1478 (p.13, the date altered to 1477 by a later hand)
    • (fol.21v)
    • (rubric) Incipiunt statute magis novissima dicte civitatis Pisarum edicta sub die xx mensis Maii MDXIIII more Florentino et 1515 stilo Pisano
    • Note: In Latin and Italian, with additions (fol.26) 1528–32
    • (fol.27v)
    • Note: Copies from legal documents made in 1566-7. 16th cent.

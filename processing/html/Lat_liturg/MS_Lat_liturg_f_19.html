History

Origin:
14th century, end
English, Norwich (?)

Provenance and Acquisition: 'Dedicatio ecclesie Norwici' (Cal., 24 Sept.) Norwich Cathedral Library (?) pressmark 'a xxiij', 15th century 'Dame Thomas Hughson est huius libri posessor'. Her husband was sacristan of Norwich Cathedral in 1547, and rector of St. Michael at Pleas in 1560. (Blomfield, Topographical Hist. of Norfolk IV 7, 327). 'Katherine Corbet', 16th century Bishop Lowth (?) (1710-87) Miss A. S. Bourne Given to Charles Martin, 1872 Given to the Bodleian by Mrs. M. Martin, 1918.

      20 long lines

    Good border, initials. Miniature on fol. 1 added 16th century. Fol. 38v, indulgence of Pope Sixtus IV (1471-1484). (P&A ii. 375)

    One miniature (fol.2) showing David at prayer, with gold initial and partial border of vine scroll design, other initials gold on blue and rose grounds, or plain red, blue and gold; some headings in gold

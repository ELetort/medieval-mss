History

Origin:
15th century, late - 16th century, early
German

Provenance and Acquisition: Dominican origin is indicated by the double invocation of St. Dominic in the litany (fol. 58v) and a reference to the two feasts of Petrus Martyr (fol. 115). Binding suggests the Dominican convent of Esslingen, Würtemberg; bought in 1960.

  • Decoration:

    Fine miniature of the Crucifixion, pasted on to fol.33v. Probably an early work of Ulrich Schreier, c. 1460-70. (P&A i. 157, pl. XI). Plain red and blue initials

History

Origin:
between 1495 and 1503 (main text); c. 1460-70 (inserted miniature)
Austrian, Salzburg

Provenance and Acquisition: Written for a Benedictine nunnery in the diocese of Salzburg. On fol.42v and 43 there are prayers for pope Alexander VI, 1492–1503 and Leonhardus [von Keutschach], bp. of Salsburg, 1495–1519, for ‘abbatiasam nostram’ and ‘abbatem nostrum’. Rev. N. W. Gresley Given to the Bodleian by F. Madan in 1917

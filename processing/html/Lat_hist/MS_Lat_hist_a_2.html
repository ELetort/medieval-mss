Contents

1
  • Magna tabula Glastoniensis,
  • Note: Extracts concerning the history of Glastonbury and the saints supposedly buried there, mostly from the history of John of Glastonbury, but also from William of Malmesbury's De antiquitate Glastoniensis ecclesiae. The final section, which is not in either, is pr. by J. A. Bennett, Somerset Archaeological Soc. Proc., N.S. XIV (1888), 120, with plate, also reproduced in J. Armitage Robinson, Two Glastonbury Legends, Cambridge, 1926 (plate IV). On the last leaf is a list of indulgences most of which were printed in a different order in Hearne's edition of John of Glastonbury, Oxford 1726, vol.II, 383. The tabula is believed to have been displayed in the abbey church at Glastonbury. For other such tabulae see B.Q.R. VII (1933), 326 n.5.

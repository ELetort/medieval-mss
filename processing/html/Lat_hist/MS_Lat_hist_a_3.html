  • Indenture for renewal of trade treaty between England and Burgundy.,
  • Note: Between Sir William Bardolf, locum tenens of captain of Calais, Master John Estcourt B LL., Richard Bokelonde, victualler of Calais, Master William Bray, clerk, John Churche, locum-tenens of the staple and John Pykering, ambassadors of England, and the commissioners of Flanders, July 14, 1419. The six red seals of the English ambassadors are on parchment tongues.

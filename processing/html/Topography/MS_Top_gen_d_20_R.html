  • Note: The manors are 'Foresta', Chillerton (Selerton) [cf. P.R.O. Ministers' Accounts 984/1], Shorwell, Bowcombe, Wootton [Wodinton], Appleford [in Godshill] (Appeltreford), Newport, Yarmouth (Ermue), Lymington Burg', Lymington, Pann (Penna), Newtown [Isle of Wight], Honiton, Burg' de Honiton, Hardisworthy (Hardewik), Botesford [Devon], Sevenhampton and Worth, Stratton [Wilts], Kennington [Berks], Constabularius, Whitchurch [Oxon], Craft and Crewkerne (Cruk') [Dorset]. At the end is attached a grant of Isabella to the Abbey of Montebourgh in Normandy.

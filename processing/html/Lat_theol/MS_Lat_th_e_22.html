MS. containing (fol.133, 133v) part of the ‘Exhortatio ad presbyteros’ ascribed to pope Eutychianus (P.L. 5, col.163–167B, ‘propter quosdam infideles’), part of the Corrector of Burchard of Worms (fol.133v) (P.L. 140, col.950C ‘sacerdos irroget penitentem’- col.951C ‘si fecisti’), and (fol.134) (P.L.col.981A ‘leguminibus siccis’ - 983D ‘pater noster decantet’) and (pastedown and fol.134v) unidentified penitential pieces. The section on fol.134v beg: ‘Is qui penitenciam’ is causa xxvi Quaestio VI, ch. 8 of Gratian 's Decretum.

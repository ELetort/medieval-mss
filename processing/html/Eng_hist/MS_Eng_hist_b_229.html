Contents

  • (fols. 1-10)
  • Inventory of the goods and chattels of Simon Burley in London
  • Note: (including details of goods delivered to others as security for debts)
  • (rubric) (fol. 1) …Simon de Burley ordeina affaire son Inuentorie le viij iour de Nouembre lan | …sir Symond auoit sibien a le Meawes come a Baynardes Castell en Loundres les | ...
  • Note: The heading matches that of the inventory of Burley's goods in National Archives, E 154/1/19 , as printed by M. Clarke, Fourteenth-Century Studies (Oxford, 1937), 119 n., and the present MS. appears to be a duplicate of that roll. Both rolls are unprinted although an extract, a list of twenty-one books owned by Burley, was published from the TNA document by V. J. Scattergood, 'Two medieval book lists', The Library 5th ser. 23, 237.
  • Note: Fols. 11-16, 19-30v, 31r-v, 33r are 18th (?)-19th cent. transcripts of the preceding. Fols. 16v-18v, 32r-v blank. Fols. 36r-42, 19th-cent. notes on Simon Burley and the Burley family.
  • French (later notes in Latin, French and English)

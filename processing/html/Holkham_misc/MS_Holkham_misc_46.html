  • Note: There are marginal glosses for each law in the hand of the text.

Physical Description

  • Secundo Folio: dultra mar
  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: perg

      Material Support: parchment

      Extent: ii (paper) + 149 + ii (paper). Dimensions (leaf): 290 × 210 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 205-10 × 185-90 (including marginal glosses) mm.

      Foliation: Modern pencil: i-ii, 1-151.

      Collation: 1(10, 2(8)-18(8), catchwords

    • Layout:

      Columns: 2
      Written Lines: 35
      35 written lines of main text; approx 3 lines of gloss to 2 lines of main text; in 2, plus 1 or 2 columns of gloss column(s).

  • Hand(s):

    Gothic

  • Decoration:

    Diagrams. Fleuronneé initials. (P&A i. 880)

    Spaces left blank for nitials at bginnings of books, smaller initials blue with red and red with blue penwork. In the lists of capitula titles of books are enclosed in circles of blue and red.

  • Binding:

    Typical Holkham binding of polished brown leather; the spine lettered in gilt 'DE LOS | ENSINAMENTOS | DE LAS LEES || M.S. | ESPAGNOL'; joints repaired.

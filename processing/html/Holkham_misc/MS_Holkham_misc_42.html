Physical Description

    • Layout:

      Columns: 2
      Written Lines: 27 28
      27-8 written lines per page; in 2 columns.

  • Hand(s):

    Neat medium grade gothic bookhand, by more than one scribe.

  • Decoration:

    Pen-flourishes on fols. 120r and 122v are in the form of fish and a human head.

  • Binding:

    Bound in typical Holkham polished brown leather, the boards framed in gilt and with the Coke crest stamped in the centre of the upper cover.

Contents

1
  • Legal cases,
  • Note: Pleas extracted from the plea rolls, for the years 14 Edw. I (1286) to 16 Edw. II (1323), in no apparent order, consisting of cases in King's Bench, common pleas and pleas before itinerant justices. In many cases the number of the roll is given.
  • Latin
2
  • (fols. 276r-)
  • Note: The sentence of exile pronounced against Hugh Despenser the younger in parliament (added)
  • French
3
  • (fols. 276v-278r)
  • Note: Index, in the hand of Sir Edward Coke, who also added some marginal notes throughout the manuscript.

Physical Description

  • Secundo Folio: presul pontifex
  • Format: codex
    • Support: Material: chart

      Material Support: Paper Watermark: mermaid, cf. Briquet, no. 13888 Watermark: eagle in circle, cf. Briquet, no. 202 Watermark: cross-bow, cf. Briquet, no. 746

      Extent: iii (modern paper) + 175 + iii (modern paper) leaves. Dimensions (leaf): 215 × 165 mm.
      Dimensions (ruled): 150 × 100-5 mm.

      Foliation: The main text with 18th-century (?) ink foliation: 1-175; the endleaves foliated in modern pencil: i-iii and 176-178.

      Collation: 1(10)-17(10), 18(6)catchwords

    • Layout:

      Written Lines: 24
      Frame ruling (?) not visible. 24 lines of text per page.

  • Hand(s):

    Written in Humanistic italic script, by three hands: (i) fols. 1r-33r; (ii) 33r-39v; (iii) fols. 40r-175r.

  • Decoration:

    Plain red initials

  • Binding:

    Brown polished and diced leather over pasteboards; the upper cover stamped in gilt with the Coke crest; the spine lettered in gilt 'BESSARIONIS | CARD. | OPUSCULA || M.S.'

History

Origin:
Italian, Rome
1467

Provenance and Acquisition: Written in Rome in 1467, with a colophon: 'Rome pridie nonas Quintiles anno salutis MoCCCCoLXVIIo pontificis vero maximi Pauli secundi Veneti tercio' (fol. 175r); and inscribed in a more formal hand 'Cardinalis Nicenus de Spiritus Sancti gratia opuscula' (fol. 175v). Bookplate of ' THOMAS WILLIAM COKE ' (1754-1842), and signed 'Ths. Wilm. Coke' (fol. 1r); formerly Norfolk, Holkham Hall, MS. 117.

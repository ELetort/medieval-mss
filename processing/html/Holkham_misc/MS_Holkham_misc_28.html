History

Origin:
Italian, Venice
15th century

Provenance and Acquisition: Written in Venice; still in Italy, in the 17th and 18th centuries. On fol. 76-81 is an account in Italian, 17th.-cent., of the conspiracy of Tiepolo and Querini. Bookplate of ' THOMAS WILLIAM COKE ', formerly Norfolk, Holkham Hall, MS. 497.

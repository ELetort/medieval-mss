History

Origin:
German
12th century, first half

Provenance and Acquisition: There are marginal notes in various Italian hands of the 13th-15th centuries. A note on the philosophical works of Cicero on fol. 1r has been erased and re-written on fol. iii verso in a 15th-century humanist hand, headed by 'ies'. Giuseppe Valletta (1636-1714), Naples (on whom see Hassall, 1970, 22, 22 n. 9), signed 'I. V.' (fol. iii verso). Bookplate of 'THOMAS WILLIAM COKE', formerly Norfolk, Holkham Hall, MS. 380.
